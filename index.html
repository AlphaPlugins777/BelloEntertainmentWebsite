Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you book.

How many hours of coverage are included?

Every package includes 4 hours of coverage. Need more time? Additional hours are available as an add-on, and we'll finalize your exact timeline together during planning.

Do you act as MC and make announcements?

Yes — every package includes professional MC and hosting. We handle introductions, announcements, and keeping your timeline on track so the night flows effortlessly.

Can we pick the music and a do-not-play list?

Absolutely. You'll share your must-plays, special moments, and any do-not-play songs through our planning portal — and we read the room live to keep the dance floor full.

What happens if there's a technical issue?

We bring backup equipment to every event and plan for contingencies, so your celebration never skips a beat.

How do we reserve our date?

Send an inquiry through the form below, we'll set up a quick consultation to talk through your vision, and a signed agreement plus deposit locks in your date.

How far in advance should we book?

Popular dates book months ahead — especially spring and fall weekends. The earlier you reach out, the better the chance we can hold your date.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the tri-state area and surrounding region. Planning something a little further out? Just ask in your inquiry — we're always happy to travel for the right event.
